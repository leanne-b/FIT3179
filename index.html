Most Expensive Melbourne Suburbs

This interactive visualisation explores the top five most expensive surburbs in Victoria to purchase a home. It explores the trends of the median house price and most expensive suburbs over time (2012-2022). Also looks into the demographics living in the most expensive suburbs.

It is a common theme to see that the most expensive suburbs are in close proximity to the Melbourne CBD and located in Melbourne's east.

Top 5 Suburbs

The most expensive suburbs to live in Melbourne in 2022 are:

  1. Toorak
    $6,200,000
  2. Canterbury
    $3,490,000
  3. Brighton
    $3,450,000
  4. East Melbourne
    $3,300,000
  5. Malvern
    $3,020,000

The chloropleth map uses colours to represent the median price range each suburb falls under. Hover on an area to see a tooltip with further details of the median price. The top five most expensive suburbs are labelled on the map.

Slide the year slider to see how top five most expensive suburbs change over time and the median prices of Melbourne suburbs. years. Zoom into the map using the zoom slider. Turn off and on labels but clicking on the Show Top 5 Most Expensive suburbs.
